# The Charter of Perpetual Consent
### Protocol of Living Governance

**Preamble: The Sovereignty of the Living**

We, the people—the collective body of currently living, conscious, and consenting individuals—do hereby declare that political sovereignty resides exclusively in the present, verifiable will of the living. We reject the premise that laws are derived from the accumulated intent of the past, nor are they bound by the legacy of the deceased. All law must be rooted in, and continuously verifiable by, the immediate and informed consent of the governed. This Charter is the foundational protocol governing the creation, maintenance, and dissolution of all legal obligations within our collective society.

***

### Article I: Foundational Axioms (The Immutable Protocol)

**Section 1: The Primacy of Life**
The absolute and undeniable right of the living to define their own civic and legal obligations supersedes any historical precedent, assumed right, or external mandate. The law is always contingent upon the will of the living.

**Section 2: The Right to Genesis and Amendment**
Every eligible, conscious citizen holds the inherent and unconditional right to propose any amendment to the body of law, or to introduce entirely new statutes. No legislative body, living or conceptual, may claim this right unilaterally.

**Section 3: The Doctrine of Automatic Cessation**
No law shall hold enduring power by default. If, at any point, the verifiable support for a law falls to or below the threshold of the immediate majority, that law shall automatically cease to be binding law. Repeal is not an act of repeal; it is an act of collective expiry.

***

### Article II: The Architecture of Consent (The Statute Book as Living Tissue)

**Section 1: The Consent Registry**
All existing laws shall be housed within a transparent, universally accessible **Consent Registry**. This Registry is the single source of truth regarding the legal order, displaying:

1.  The law’s full plain-language text.
2.  A concise summary of its purpose and mechanisms.
3.  The real-time, verifiable support count, the required majority threshold (N/2 + 1), and the current gap between the two.
4.  A timeline of all historical support fluctuations, demonstrating the law's stability and fragility over time.

**Section 2: The Three Stances**
For every law and every proposal, every eligible voter must declare one of three statuses:

*   **Support:** An active, affirmative declaration of consent that contributes to the majority threshold.
*   **Opposition:** A reasoned rejection of the law’s continuing mandate.
*   **Abstention:** A declaration that the law is neither accepted nor rejected by the individual voter, but remains recorded in the registry.

**Section 3: The Protocol of Proposal**
For any proposal to attain the status of law, it must achieve **a verifiable, demonstrable majority consensus (>50%)** among the current pool of voters, thereby proving its mandate from the collective will.

***

### Article III: The Mechanisms of Governance

**Section 1: The Role of Expertise (The Watchee Protocol)**
The Protocol shall incorporate a mechanism for aggregated expert and community intelligence. Citizens shall be able to "watch" the registered stances and stated reasons of recognized experts, organizations, and community leaders. This function provides citizens with transparent social navigation, allowing them to weigh the collective opinion and reasoned arguments of various authoritative voices before casting their own, sovereign vote.

**Section 2: Mandatory Clarity and Transparency**
Any law created must include a mandatory, linked public argument space. This space requires the original proposer to provide not only the legal text but also a detailed, accessible impact assessment, including:

*   The intended benefits.
*   The projected costs and burdens.
*   The specific group(s) of people who will be most affected, and why.

**Section 3: The Governance Principle**
The law is not defined by its text, but by the continuous, verifiable, and collective consent of the living. The moment the foundational protocols are threatened, the law expires.
